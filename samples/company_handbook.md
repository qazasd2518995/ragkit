# Acme Corp Employee Handbook (excerpt)

## Remote Work Policy
Employees may work remotely up to three days per week. Fully remote
arrangements require approval from a department head and are reviewed every
six months. Core collaboration hours are 10:00–15:00 in the employee's local
time zone.

## Paid Time Off
Full-time employees accrue 20 days of paid vacation per year, plus 10 public
holidays. Unused vacation of up to 5 days may be carried over into the next
calendar year; anything beyond that is forfeited on December 31st.

## Refund and Reimbursement
Work-related expenses are reimbursed within 14 business days of submitting a
receipt through the expense portal. Software subscriptions under $50/month can
be approved by a team lead; anything above requires finance approval.

## Security
All laptops must use full-disk encryption and a password manager. Reusing
passwords across services is prohibited. Suspected phishing emails should be
reported to security@acme.example within one hour.
